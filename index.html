CHIPS

INGREDIENTS

  • Potatoes,Large,Washed 1 kg
  • Cornflour 1 tbsp
  • Rice Flour 2 tbsp
  • Gram Floor (besan) 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil as required for deep frying

Buy These Ingredients Now

COOKING DIRECTION

  • Boil potatoes for 5 minutes.
  • Peel and cut into long thin chips.
  • Put the cornflour , rice , flour , gram flour and salt in a thick brown paper bag and shake to mix thoroughly.
  • Then put chips in the bad and shake to coat the chips with the cornflour mixture.
  • Heat oil in a large wok till very hot , add chips , stir and turn the heat down to medium.
  • Fry the chips , remove with golden brown to absorbent kitchen paper towel.
